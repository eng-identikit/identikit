Identikit

An Industrial Autonomic Engine for Digital Twin Orchestration and Asset Administration Shell (AAS) Governance.

Digital Twin Ecosystem

Seamless Interoperability

Identikit is a comprehensive toolkit designed to bridge the gap between physical assets and their digital representations. By leveraging the Asset Administration Shell (AAS) standard, it enables secure, semantically-rich, and autonomic management of industrial data across the entire product lifecycle.

Core Modules

The Identikit Suite

Modular components designed to visualize, model, and connect industrial assets using international standards.

Modeling — AAS Studio

AAS Studio

A sophisticated visual environment for authoring AAS models. Define Submodels and ConceptDescriptions with precision, ensuring full alignment with IDTA specifications without manual coding.

Connectivity — AAS Bridge

AAS Bridge

The industrial integration layer. It synchronizes live telemetry from OPC-UA and MQTT brokers directly into the Digital Twin, ensuring the AAS reflects the physical state in real-time.

Intelligence — Chatbot AAS

Chatbot AAS

Powered by LLM Agents, this conversational interface allows engineers to interrogate asset data and automatically generate AAS structures from technical documentation.

Security & Infrastructure

Governance & Access Control

Managing trust and visibility in complex supply chains through standardized security protocols.

Security — Access Control Manager

AAS Access Control Manager

Implements robust Attribute-Based Access Control (ABAC) to govern API access. Protect sensitive industrial data by defining granular permissions for external stakeholders.

Discovery — DPP Registry & Repository

DPP Registry & Repository

The backbone of the Digital Product Passport initiative. Centralize your AAS instances to ensure full traceability and compliance with emerging circular economy regulations.

Data Transformation

Semantic Interoperability

Converting industrial specifications into actionable knowledge graphs.

Mapper AAS-RDF

Automatically transforms AAS models into RDF/Linked Data. This enables cross-domain reasoning and advanced SPARQL queries, allowing Identikit to integrate assets into broader corporate Knowledge Graphs.
